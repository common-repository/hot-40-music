The Hot40Music.com plugin lets you stream  non-stop music to your visitors.

== Description ==

The Hot 40 Music plugin is a simple plugin-widget from Greece's leading web radio Hot40Music.com for bloggers/websites to include non-stop music on their websites. Hot40Music.com is not responsible for any charges in other countries that may be applied by their local record license suppliers. 

The Hot40Music.com plugin plays non stop international hits.  More options and more channels to come soon. 

== Installation ==

1. Upload the Hot40Music folder to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
1. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
1. Place the widget in your side bar.  Your sidebar must have a minimum width of 300px otherwise it will not show properly.
